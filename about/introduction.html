Introduction

LEAP Lab (Learning Engineering, AI, and NLP) is a research group in the Department of Computer Science and Software Engineering at Korea University Sejong Campus, led by Prof. Unggi Lee.

Our lab focuses on the intersection of artificial intelligence and education. We pursue cutting-edge research in Generative AI, Large Language Models, Pedagogical Alignment, Knowledge Tracing, and Robotics (VLA) to build intelligent systems that enhance teaching and learning experiences.

Research Areas

  • Generative AI in Education
  • Pedagogical Alignment for LLMs
  • Knowledge Tracing & Learning Analytics
  • Multimodal AI for Education
  • Robotics & Vision-Language-Action (VLA) Models
  • AI-Powered Educational Tools
