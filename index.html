Who We're Looking For

Our lab sits at the intersection of Education and Technology. We recruit two types of students:

Education Track

Background in education or learning sciences. Teaching experience (e.g., K-12) is a plus.

ML/Tech Track

Background in ML, NLP, or software engineering. Research on LLM (fine-tuning, alignment, knowledge tracing) and VLA (embodied AI, educational robotics).

Regardless of your initial track, all members are expected to develop competency in both perspectives before graduation. Learn more →

Research Highlights

Generative AI in Education

Designing and developing generative AI systems for teaching and learning.

Pedagogical Alignment

Aligning large language models with pedagogical principles and educational goals.

Large Language Models

Developing and fine-tuning LLMs for educational applications.

Knowledge Tracing

Modeling student knowledge states using deep learning approaches.

Robotics (VLA)

Vision-Language-Action models for educational robotics and embodied AI.
